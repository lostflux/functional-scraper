NASA scientists are also working on ways to repel dust particles using a small electric charge, sort of like static electricity. The device pushes very small dust particles away and also charges larger particles, enabling them to be attracted to an electrically charged plate to keep the dust out. The device could be scaled to different sizes, but it is intended for smaller areas that are especially sensitive to dust, such as a housing for bearings or a rover wheel shaft.
