Emerging fields such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Data Science, and deep learning have become essential in the drive for innovation, efficiency, and growth across various industries globally. In recent years, AI has made significant strides in transforming business processes, fostering expansion, and having us rethink the utilisation of these technologies. As a result, companies have begun integrating AI into their operations, products, and services to enhance productivity, facilitating growth.
The Deep Learning Indaba will bring together relevant stakeholders – entrepreneurs, investors, AI experts, industry leaders, and even enthusiasts – to discuss strategies, challenges, and opportunities in leveraging AI to scale up emerging businesses.
The call for applications is now open! Submit your application here.
 The submission deadline is 10 August, 2023.
