 Browne studies how surveillance technologies have objectified, categorized, and repressed black people, from the panoptic slave ships of the Middle Passage to modern policing tools deployed against protesters. Amid recent police shootings of unarmed black men, WIRED spoke with Browne about Big Tech's unconvincing support for racial justice and how sharing black pain on social media may mobilize action, but only on Silicon Valley's terms.
