How to watch AC Milan vs PSG in the US Paramount Plus is the home of all things Champions League in the US. While it’s a little smaller than other streaming services , it’s an ideal outlet for sports fans to check out. It’s worth checking out what’s new on Paramount Plus while you’re at it.
Paramount Plus costs $6 a month for the Essential plan or $12 a month for the Premium plan. If you’re solely interested in watching AC Milan vs PSG, you can do so for free via a Paramount Plus free trial which gives you free and full access to the service for seven days.

To watch the match, you need to sign up for Stan which costs from $10 per month before adding the Stan Sport add-on package for $15 a month to catch the Champions League along with rugby, and Grand Slam Tennis.
