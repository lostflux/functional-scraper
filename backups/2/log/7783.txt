The film tells the tale of Pat Solitano (Bradley Cooper), a man who loses everything after spending eight months in a state institution for a violent outburst brought on by undiagnosed bipolar disorder.
Returning home, Pat is determined to remain positive and rebuild his life, including his relationship with his wife. To do so, he enlists the help of Tiffany (Lawrence), a local girl with her own problems. But she’ll only help him reconnect with his wife if he helps her compete in a ballroom dance competition.
