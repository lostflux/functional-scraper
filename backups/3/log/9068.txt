She served as the Director of Stanford’s AI Lab from 2013 to 2018. And during her sabbatical from Stanford from January 2017 to September 2018, she was Vice President at Google and served as Chief Scientist of AI/ML at Google Cloud. Many people will recognise Dr Li as one of the creators of the ImageNet challenge and for her important work in advancing the state of the art in computer vision and machine learning.
